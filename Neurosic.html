Neurosic Intelligence

Neurosic is a research project dedicated to the study of waves and their influence on various aspects of physics. The research aims to understand and develop methods to measure and interact with specific wave patterns.

Currently, the focus is on neurowaves in the brain. The project aims to research and develop brain computer interfaces that can connect to multiple neurons, allowing interaction with and influence over these neural wave patterns.

The initiative welcomes collaboration with researchers and engineers passionate about neural wave patterns. Neurosic's published research has contributed to the understanding of cognitive processing and wave interactions.
