Protecting Your Privacy

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using any of the Services, you agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this Privacy Policy, please do not use or access the Services.

We may modify this Privacy Policy from time to time, in which case we will update the “Last Updated” date at the top of this Privacy Policy. When you use or access the Services, we collect certain categories of information about you from a variety of sources.

Some features of the Services may require you to directly provide us with certain information about yourself. If you link third-party accounts (e.g., Google or Apple) to your Votality account, we collect and use this information to maintain and manage your account.

Publicly accessible sources, such as articles or journals, may also provide information relevant to the Services. All collected data is used to provide and improve the Services, enhance safety, troubleshoot issues, and comply with applicable laws and obligations. Where applicable, we may anonymize your data to ensure it cannot be linked back to you ("Deidentified Information") and use such information for legitimate purposes, ensuring compliance with legal requirements.
